I bought one of the very last brand-new 2019 Premiere Volts last year. GM had discounted it heavily, and in combination with my dad's GM employee discount and local state incentives, I paid just a hair over $20,000 USD. I also got the faster 7.2 Kw charger, power seat, and other upgrades. I mean, you just can't beat a deal like that. I've had it just about a year now and have only put 6,000 miles on it. I still love the car. I also owned a Model-3 that I was renting out on Turo for a while, but just sold it since nobody is renting during COVID. Some people were surprised I didn't sell the Volt and keep the Tesla. But the Volt literally cost less than half what I paid for the Tesla and I like it just as much.
